Our Services

Our Specialities

Packaging Items

CarryIt provides professional packaging services to ensure the safety and security of your items during transport. We use top-of-the-line packaging materials and employ strict handling procedures to ensure that your items arrive at their destination in the same condition as they were when they left. We also offers customized packaging plans and specialized handling for fragile items to ensure the best protection for all your belongings.

Truck Ordering

CarryIt offers the option for customers to order a truck for their transportation needs. Whether it's a small truck or a large one, CarryIt's fleet of vehicles are equipped to handle and transport items of all types and sizes. Customers can choose from a range of truck options and have them delivered to the location of their choice for a convenient and efficient moving experience.

Full Truck Reservations

CarryIt offers the option for customers to reserve an entire truck for their transportation needs. This allows customers to have exclusive use of the truck and ensures that their items are transported safely and securely. This service is perfect for large-scale moves or for customers who require a significant amount of space for their items.

Slot Reservations

CarryIt offers customers the ability to reserve a portion of one of their trucks for their specific transportation needs, whether it's a small amount of items or short distance. This service is cost-effective solution for those looking to transport their items in a shared space.
